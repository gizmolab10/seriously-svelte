Welcome to Seriously

Seriously is a graph of your hierarchal data. Think: hierarchal pop-down menu, but less fussy. Browse around. Zoom your focus (hide unimportant stuff). Edit your data in comfort and instantly see the impact.

It has four areas (illustrated below): Details are at the lower left. The large area at the lower right is the graph. There are two bands at the top: controls (above) and breadcrumbs.
